Focaccia with caramelized onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds whit the festive focaccia appetizer topped whit sweetcaramelized onions, tart
cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

Ingredients


For the focaccia:
  • 4 cups (500gr) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350gr) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more fore the pan
For the topping:
  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes, (scrape rind off whitw a paring knife if desired, thought it's not
    necessary).
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
Method

Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all,
the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the
dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate
the last of the flour..
